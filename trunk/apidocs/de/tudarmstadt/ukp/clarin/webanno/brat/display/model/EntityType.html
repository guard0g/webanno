Different attributes of an Entity used for its visualisation formats. It looks like {"name":"Named Entity","type":"Named Entity","unused":true,"fgColor":"black","bgColor":"cyan", "borderColor":"green","labels":[], "children":[{"name":"LOC","type":"LOC","unused":false, "fgColor":"black","bgColor":"cyan","borderColor":"green","labels":["LOC"],"children":[], "attributes":[],"arcs":[]},....
